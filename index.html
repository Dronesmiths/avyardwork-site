Antelope Valley Yard Care: FAQ

Find expert answers to common questions about keeping your High Desert property in top shape.

Do you service Lancaster and Palmdale?

Yes. We provide full landscaping and maintenance services throughout the entire Antelope Valley region, including specific neighborhood coverage for both major cities.

Is there a contract required for recurring service?

Never. We believe in earning your business with every visit. You can pause or cancel your recurring yard maintenance at any time with no penalty.

What happens if I have heavy weeds?

For overgrown yards, we typically perform a one-time "Reset" service to restore the property level before transitioning you to a standard, affordable weekly maintenance schedule.

Do you handle irrigation repairs?

We do. We specialize in High Desert irrigation solutions, fixing drip systems and sprinkler heads to maximize water efficiency during our harsh summers.

How do I get a quote for my yard?

Simply call or text us at 661-498-4444. We can often provide an estimate the same day based on your property size and service needs.

What is included in a standard mow?

A standard visit includes professional mowing of all grass areas, vertical edging of driveways and walkways, and a full blow-off of all hard surfaces.
