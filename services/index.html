Yard Care FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our landscaping and maintenance services in the Antelope Valley.

What services are included in your standard maintenance?

Our recurring maintenance visits typically include professional mowing, sidewalk edging, line trimming around obstacles, and blowing clippings from all hard surfaces.

Do you handle irrigation repairs?

Yes. We offer expert irrigation diagnostics and repairs for both drip systems and traditional sprinkler heads to ensure your yard stays hydrated efficiently.

Can I schedule a one-time service?

Absolutely. While we specialize in recurring care, we frequently provide one-time yard cleanups, weed removals, and landscaping project resets.

Is your crew insured?

Yes. AV Yard Work is a professionally run, insured business. We take every precaution to ensure the safety of your property and our staff.

How do I pay for recurring service?

We offer easy, automated billing for our recurring clients, so you never have to worry about checks or cash for your weekly yard care.

What happens in the event of bad weather?

In the rare event of extreme desert high winds or rain, we proactively reschedule your service for the next earliest available day to ensure quality results.
