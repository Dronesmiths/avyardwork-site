Tree Trimming FAQs

Common questions about professional tree trimming and pruning services in the Antelope Valley.

When is the best time to trim trees in the High Desert?

Late winter to early spring (February-March) is ideal for most trees, before new growth begins. However, we can safely trim year-round, and dead or hazardous branches should be removed immediately regardless of season.

How often should trees be trimmed?

Most mature trees benefit from professional trimming every 3-5 years. Fast-growing species or trees near structures may need more frequent attention. We can assess your specific trees and recommend a schedule.

Can you trim trees near power lines?

We can safely trim branches approaching power lines, but we maintain proper clearances and follow utility company guidelines. For branches directly touching lines, the utility company must be contacted first.

Do you remove the trees completely, or just trim them?

We specialize in trimming and pruning to maintain healthy, beautiful trees. For complete tree removal, we can provide referrals to licensed tree removal specialists with the heavy equipment required.

Will trimming harm my tree?

When done properly by trained professionals, pruning improves tree health, structure, and longevity. We follow ISA standards and never remove more than 25% of a tree's canopy in a single season.

What happens to the branches and debris?

All branches, wood, and debris are completely removed from your property and properly disposed of. We leave your yard clean and ready to enjoyâ€”no cleanup required from you.
