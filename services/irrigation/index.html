Irrigation Services FAQs

Common questions about irrigation repair, installation, and water-efficient upgrades in the Antelope Valley.

How do I know if my irrigation system has a leak?

Common signs include unusually high water bills, soggy spots in your yard, low water pressure, or zones that won't turn on. We offer comprehensive diagnostics to identify hidden leaks.

Should I use drip irrigation or sprinklers in the High Desert?

Drip irrigation is ideal for desert gardens, xeriscapes, and shrubsâ€”it's 90% more efficient than sprinklers. Traditional sprinklers work better for lawns. We can design a hybrid system for optimal results.

Can you convert my old system to a smart controller?

Absolutely. Smart controllers adjust watering based on weather, soil moisture, and plant typesâ€” reducing water usage by 30-50%. Most upgrades can be completed in a single visit.

How often should irrigation systems be serviced?

We recommend professional inspection twice a yearâ€”spring startup and fall winterization. This prevents costly repairs and ensures optimal efficiency throughout the year.

Do you offer emergency irrigation repair?

Yes. We understand that broken irrigation can mean dying plants and wasted water. We offer same-day or next-day emergency service for urgent repairs.

How much can I save with water-efficient upgrades?

Most homeowners see 30-50% reduction in outdoor water usage after upgrading to smart controllers and low-flow nozzles. In the High Desert, this translates to significant annual savings.
