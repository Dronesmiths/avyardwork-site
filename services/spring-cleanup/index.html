Spring Cleanup FAQs

Common questions about spring yard preparation in Palmdale and Lancaster.

When should I schedule spring cleanup in the Antelope Valley?

The ideal time is March through early April, before temperatures consistently hit 80Â°F. This gives your lawn time to recover and strengthen before summer heat arrives.

Do I need aeration every spring?

In the High Desert, annual spring aeration is highly recommended. Our alkaline soil compacts easily, restricting water absorption and root growth. Aeration dramatically improves lawn health.

What's the difference between spring and fall cleanup?

Spring cleanup focuses on preparing for growth (aeration, fertilization, weed prevention). Fall cleanup focuses on winterization (debris removal, final mowing, protecting plants from cold).

Can you do spring cleanup and start regular mowing?

Absolutely! Many customers start with spring cleanup, then transition to weekly or bi-weekly mowing service. We'll keep your yard looking great all season.
