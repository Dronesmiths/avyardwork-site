Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in garden care services?

Garden care typically includes weeding, pruning, fertilization, planting, soil conditioning, and general bed maintenance to keep plants healthy and attractive.

How often should garden care be scheduled?

Most gardens benefit from monthly or seasonal service, depending on plant types, irrigation, and time of year. We can recommend a schedule that fits your landscape.

Do you provide fertilization and soil care?

Yes. We apply appropriate fertilizers and soil amendments to promote healthy growth while avoiding overfeeding or waste.

Can you help restore overgrown garden beds?

Absolutely. Our garden care services are ideal for cleaning up neglected beds, removing weeds, and restoring structure and beauty to your landscape.
