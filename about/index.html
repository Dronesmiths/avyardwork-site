The Founder's Story

Rooted in Reliability

AV Yard Work began with a simple observation: the Antelope Valley needed a landscaping service that combined high-end design with rock-solid reliability. Too often, homeowners were forced to choose between artistry and consistency.

We set out to change that standard. Starting with a single truck and a passion for xeriscaping, we've grown into the region's premier choice for drought-tolerant transformations. We understand the wind, the heat, and the soil of the AV better than anyone because this is our home.

Our team is vetted, trained, and uniformed. When we step onto your property, we treat it with the same respect we would our own.
